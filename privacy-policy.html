Privacy Policy - Home Decoration Gallery

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

?? Camera Permission Usage

Why We Need Camera Access: Our Home Decoration Gallery app requires camera permission exclusively for admin use. Only the administrator can capture and upload photos directly from the device camera to populate the home decoration gallery with content.

How We Use It: The camera is used exclusively by the admin to:

  • Capture decoration photos for gallery display
  • Upload photos to different decoration categories
  • Create a professional gallery of decoration ideas
  • Share decoration inspiration with users

Important: Regular users cannot access the camera or upload photos. Only the authenticated administrator has camera access privileges.

?? User Access and Permissions

Admin vs User Access:

  • Admin Only: Camera access and photo upload capabilities
  • Regular Users: Can view and browse the gallery only
  • No User Photos: Customer photos are not accessed or stored
  • Secure Separation: Admin functions are completely separate from user data

?? Data Collection and Use

Information We Collect:

  • Admin Photos: Photos captured and uploaded by administrator only
  • Metadata: Upload timestamps and category information
  • Device Info: Basic device information for app optimization
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app performance data

What We DON'T Collect:

  • No Customer Photos: We do not access or store user photos
  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No Location Data: We don't access or store location information
  • No Contact Access: We don't access your contacts
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Admin photos are not sold or shared with third parties

?? Data Storage and Security

Admin Content Only: Only administrator-uploaded photos are stored in the app. User photos are never accessed, stored, or processed.

Security: Admin functions are protected by authentication and standard Android security practices.

?? User Rights and Control

Regular User Rights:

  • View Only: Browse and view the decoration gallery
  • No Camera Access: Camera permission is not used for regular users
  • No Photo Upload: Cannot upload or modify photos
  • Privacy Protected: Personal photos remain private and inaccessible

Admin Rights:

  • Camera Access: Can capture and upload decoration photos
  • Content Management: Can manage gallery content
  • Authentication Required: Admin login required for camera access

?? Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.
