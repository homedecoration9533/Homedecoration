Privacy Policy - Home Decoration Gallery

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

📷 Camera Permission Usage

Why We Need Camera Access: Our Home Decoration Gallery app requires camera permission to allow you to capture and upload photos directly from your device camera for home decoration inspiration and sharing.

How We Use It: The camera is used exclusively within the app to:

  • Capture new photos for your home decoration projects
  • Upload photos to different decoration categories
  • Create a personal gallery of your decoration ideas
  • Share decoration inspiration with others

🔒 Data Collection and Use

Information We Collect:

  • Photos: Images you capture or upload through the app
  • Metadata: Upload timestamps and category information
  • Device Info: Basic device information for app optimization
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app performance data

What We DON'T Collect:

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No Location Data: We don't access or store location information
  • No Contact Access: We don't access your contacts
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your photos are not sold or shared with third parties

💾 Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All photos are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to external servers without your explicit consent.

Security: Your photos are protected by your device's security measures. The app uses standard Android security practices to keep your data safe.

🔐 User Rights and Control

Your Rights:

  • Access: You can access all your photos at any time
  • Delete: You can delete any photos you don't want
  • Export: You can export your photos from the app
  • Permission Control: You can revoke camera permission at any time
  • Data Request: You can request a copy of your data

👥 Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.
